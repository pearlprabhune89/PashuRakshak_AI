Disease Library

Detectable Conditions

Our CNN model is trained to detect and differentiate these critical bovine diseases.

🔴
Lumpy Skin Disease (LSD)
Highly Contagious Common
Viral disease causing nodular skin lesions, fever, and reduced milk production. Spread by insects and contact. Can lead to severe economic loss if undetected.
🦷
Foot & Mouth Disease (FMD)
Highly Contagious Manageable
Causes painful blisters on mouth, tongue, hooves, and teats. Severe lameness and inability to eat. Spreads rapidly through contaminated feed, water, and contact.
🥛
Mastitis
Very Common Treatable
Inflammation of the udder caused by bacterial infection. Main cause of reduced milk yield and quality. Early detection through visual inspection and milk changes is critical.
