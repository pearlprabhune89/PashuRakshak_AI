Intelligent Decision Engine

Before draining, the system checks real-time sewer capacity. If sewers are at risk of overflow, water is automatically redirected — preventing secondary flooding downstream.

♻️

Rainwater Harvesting Integration

Excess floodwater is routed to underground storage tanks, soak pits, or rainwater harvesting systems — turning a crisis into a resource for dry-season water use.

☀️

Solar + Battery Independence

The entire system runs on solar energy with intelligent battery backup — ensuring full operation even during the extended power outages that accompany severe storms.

🔄

Fully Autonomous Operation

From detection to drainage, zero human intervention is required. The system acts in seconds — not hours — eliminating the bottleneck of manual emergency response.
