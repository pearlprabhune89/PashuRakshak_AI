Welcome to History Explorer

History Explorer is an educational website that helps students understand important historical events, monuments, and timelines in a simple and visual way. This project is made using only HTML and CSS.

What You Will Learn

  • Major events in Indian and World History
  • Important monuments and their importance
  • How monuments were built in ancient times
  • Difference between past and present condition of monuments

Why History is Important?

History teaches us about our culture, heritage, achievements, and mistakes. By learning history, we can understand how civilizations developed and how our present world was shaped.
